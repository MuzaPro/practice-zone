Behind the Design

This design system represents our approach to Computational Brutalism - where scientific precision meets radical digital design. Built for researchers, scientists, and deep-tech innovators who refuse to compromise between authority and visual innovation.

Every element is designed to suggest computational activity, data processing, and the methodical precision of advanced research facilities while maintaining uncompromising readability and accessibility.

Dark Mode

Computational Terminal Aesthetic

Dark mode transforms the design into a computational terminal experience - perfect for researchers working late nights or presenting in dark conference rooms.

The brutalist aesthetic becomes even more striking with inverted contrast, creating that authentic "working inside a quantum computer" feeling.

◐

Adaptive Mesh Background

The animated mesh network automatically adapts to the theme - subtle dark connections in light mode, bright connections in dark mode. Perfect for that data-processing laboratory aesthetic.

/* CSS Custom Properties for Theme Switching */ :root { --bg-primary: var(--white); --text-primary: var(--gray-900); --border-primary: var(--black); } [data-theme="dark"] { --bg-primary: var(--gray-900); --text-primary: var(--white); --border-primary: var(--white); }

Try it now! Click the theme toggle in the top-right corner to experience how the entire design system adapts while maintaining its brutalist authority.

Design Philosophy

"Where scientific precision meets radical digital design. Every pixel serves both function and authority."

Computational Brutalism isn't just an aesthetic choice—it's a statement about how scientific content should be presented. Bold, unapologetic, precise, and beautiful. Perfect for organizations that refuse to choose between credibility and innovation.
